About {{ site_settings.school_name|default:"Our School" }}

The school is located in Giwa LGA of Kaduna, Nigeria. It was founded by Imam Suleiman Madawaki Giwa in 2009 and later donated to the community.

The school runs various sections apart from western education. It also has an Islamic School in the afternoon, including Arabic classes for married women and mass education learners.

Our school is renowned for its vibrant community spirit, fostering a supportive environment where students achieve their fullest potential and gain essential life skills to become responsible citizens.
